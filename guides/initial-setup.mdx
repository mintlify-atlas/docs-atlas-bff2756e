After installing NeoSC, complete the following steps to create your first network and connect your devices.

<Note>
  The dashboard is also accessible via the NeoSC system tray icon — you do not need to open a browser manually.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the NeoSC dashboard">
    Navigate to [https://localhost:8080](https://localhost:8080) in your browser, or click the NeoSC icon in the system tray to open the dashboard directly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up using your email address or an SSO provider supported by your organization. After registering:

    1. Verify your email address using the link sent to your inbox.
    2. Set up two-factor authentication.

    <Tip>
      Set up two-factor authentication immediately after creating your account. Using an authenticator app or hardware key significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first network">
    Once logged in, you will be prompted to create a network:

    1. Enter a descriptive **Name** for the network (for example, `Production Network`).
    2. Add an optional **Description** to identify the network's purpose.
    3. Click **Create Network**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add peers">
    With your network created, add the devices that should be part of it:

    1. Go to the **Peers** section in the sidebar.
    2. Click **Add Peer**.
    3. Select the peer type: **Linux**, **Windows**, **macOS**, or **Mobile**.
    4. Download the NeoSC agent for the target device and follow the on-screen installation instructions.

    Repeat this process for each device you want to connect to the network.
  </Step>
</Steps>
