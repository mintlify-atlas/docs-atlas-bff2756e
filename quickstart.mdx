This guide walks you through everything from creating your account to connecting to your virtual desktop. The entire process takes approximately 8 minutes after checkout.

<Tip>
  Choosing annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly. You can switch billing cycles at any time from your account settings.
</Tip>

## Steps

<Steps>

  <Step title="Start your free trial">
    Go to the WinDesk Cloud sign-up page and create your account. No credit card is required to start.

    You receive a **72-hour free trial** on any plan, giving you full access to your chosen desktop environment before you're billed.

    <Note>
      WinDesk Cloud is currently in beta. Trial desktops have the same performance and security as paid plans.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Select the plan that fits your workload. All plans include Windows 11, zero-trust network access, and the 8-minute provisioning SLA.

    | Plan | Price | vCPU | RAM | Storage |
    |---|---|---|---|---|
    | Starter | $29/mo | 2 cores | 4 GB | 60 GB |
    | Business | $59/mo | 4 cores | 8 GB | 100 GB |
    | Enterprise | $129/mo | 8 cores | 16 GB | 250 GB |

    The **Business** plan is the most popular choice for teams needing a capable everyday desktop. If you need more resources than the Enterprise plan offers, you can increase them in the configurator.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your desktop">
    After selecting a plan, the interactive configurator at [/configure](/pricing/configure) lets you fine-tune your hardware resources and deployment region.

    **Hardware resources**

    Drag the sliders to set your preferred values:

    | Resource | Minimum | Maximum |
    |---|---|---|
    | vCPU | 2 cores | 16 cores |
    | RAM | 4 GB | 64 GB |
    | Storage | 60 GB | 500 GB |

    **Deployment region**

    Choose the region closest to your users to minimize latency:

    - 🇲🇽 Mexico
    - 🇺🇸 USA
    - 🇪🇺 Europe
    - Bare Metal (dedicated hardware, available on Enterprise)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your operating system">
    Select the operating system for your virtual desktop. The OS is installed automatically during provisioning.

    | OS | Price | Notes |
    |---|---|---|
    | Windows 11 | Included | Full desktop, Office-compatible, RDP access |
    | Ubuntu 22.04 | +$5/mo | Linux environment with dev tools, noVNC access |
    | macOS Sonoma | +$15/mo | macOS environment, browser-based access |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add optional add-ons">
    Enhance your desktop with optional services. Add-ons can be added or removed at any time from your dashboard.

    | Add-on | Price | Description |
    |---|---|---|
    | Automated Backups | +$15/mo | Daily snapshots with one-click restore |
    | Advanced SSO | +$25/mo | Connect Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, or any OIDC provider |
    | Private Relay | +$20/mo | Dedicated relay node for lower-latency connections |
    | Security Suite | +$30/mo | Extended audit logging, IP allowlisting, and session recording |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Review your order summary and complete payment. WinDesk Cloud uses Stripe for secure payment processing.

    After checkout, provisioning starts automatically:

    1. Virtual machine is created in your selected region
    2. Operating system is installed and configured
    3. Zero-trust network tunnel is established
    4. Connection credentials are delivered to your account

    Your desktop will be ready in approximately **8 minutes**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect to your desktop">
    Once provisioning is complete, open your dashboard and click **Connect**. You can access your desktop three ways:

    **Browser (recommended for first access)**

    Click the connection link in your dashboard. Your desktop opens in a new browser tab — no software installation required.

    **NeoSC client (recommended for daily use)**

    Install the NeoSC client for your platform to get the best performance and lowest latency:

    <CodeGroup>

    ```bash Linux (Ubuntu / Debian)
    sudo apt-get update && sudo apt-get install -y curl gnupg lsb-release
    curl https://repo.neosc.io/ubuntu/KEY.gpg | sudo apt-key add -
    echo "deb https://repo.neosc.io/ubuntu $(lsb_release -cs) main" | sudo tee /etc/apt/sources.list.d/neosc.list
    sudo apt-get update && sudo apt-get install -y neosc
    ```

    ```bash macOS (Homebrew)
    brew tap neosc/tap
    brew install neosc
    brew services start neosc
    ```

    ```bash Windows (winget)
    winget install NeoSC.NeoSC
    ```

    </CodeGroup>

    See the [installation guides](/guides/installation-linux) for full platform-specific instructions.

    **RDP (Windows desktops only)**

    Use any RDP client with the credentials provided in your dashboard. The connection is tunneled securely through the zero-trust network layer — no firewall changes required.
  </Step>

</Steps>
