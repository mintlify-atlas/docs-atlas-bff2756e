## Annual billing

Switch to annual billing and save **20% off** the monthly rate on any plan. The discount applies to the base plan price and is calculated at checkout.

| Plan       | Monthly price | Annual price (per month) | Annual savings |
| ---------- | ------------- | ------------------------ | -------------- |
| Starter    | $29           | $23.20                   | $69.60 / year  |
| Business   | $59           | $47.20                   | $141.60 / year |
| Enterprise | $129          | $103.20                  | $309.60 / year |

## Add-ons

Extend any plan with optional add-ons. Add-ons are billed at the same cycle (monthly or annual) as your base plan.

| Add-on             | Price         | What's included                              |
| ------------------ | ------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Automated Backups  | +$15 / mo     | Daily backups with 30-day retention          |
| Advanced SSO       | +$25 / mo     | SAML 2.0 and OIDC identity provider integration |
| Private Relay      | +$20 / mo     | Dedicated relay servers for your organization |
| Security Suite     | +$30 / mo     | MFA enforcement, DLP policies, threat detection |

<Note>
  Advanced SSO and Security Suite are included by default in the **Business** and **Enterprise** plans respectively. Purchasing them as add-ons unlocks the same capabilities on the Starter plan.
</Note>
