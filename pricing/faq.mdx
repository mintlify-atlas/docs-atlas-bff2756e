---
title: 'Pricing FAQ'
description: Common questions about WinDesk Cloud pricing and billing.
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans anytime?">
    Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from the admin dashboard. Changes take effect immediately — upgrades give you access to the new resources right away, and downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Yes. All plans include a **14-day free trial** with full access to every feature. No credit card is required to start the trial. If you decide not to continue, your desktops are deprovisioned automatically at the end of the trial period and no charge is applied.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express). Business and Enterprise customers can also pay by **wire transfer** or **purchase order**. Contact [billing@windesk.cloud](mailto:billing@windesk.cloud) to arrange an alternative payment method.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer volume discounts?">
    Yes. For large deployments — typically 50 or more desktops — our sales team can put together a custom pricing arrangement. [Contact sales](mailto:sales@windesk.cloud) with your expected desktop count and region to receive a quote.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a setup fee?">
    No. There are no setup fees, onboarding fees, or hidden charges. You pay only for the plan you choose, billed monthly or annually. Your first desktop can be running in approximately 8 minutes from sign-up.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What about data migration?">
    We provide **free migration assistance** for all plans. Our team will work with you to move your existing data and application configurations into WinDesk Cloud. Migrations are typically completed within one business day for Starter and Business plans, and within a few hours for Enterprise plans with a dedicated account manager.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does annual billing work?">
    When you choose annual billing, you are charged once per year for the full 12-month cost of your configuration. The annual price applies a **20% discount** to the equivalent monthly total, covering the base plan, any OS surcharge, and all add-ons. You can switch between monthly and annual billing at any time; switching to annual takes effect immediately and you receive a prorated credit for any unused monthly period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add or remove add-ons after signing up?">
    Yes. Add-ons can be enabled or disabled at any time from the admin dashboard. Enabling an add-on bills you for the remainder of the current billing period on a prorated basis. Disabling an add-on removes it immediately, and you receive a prorated credit toward your next invoice.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is pricing the same in all regions?">
    Yes. The base plan prices, OS surcharges, and add-on prices are identical across all regions — Mexico, USA (Texas), Europe (Frankfurt), and Bare Metal (OVH). Region selection affects latency and data residency, not cost.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
