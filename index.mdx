## Get up and running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your plan">
    Pick the plan that fits your team size and resource needs. Start with a 72-hour free trial — no credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your desktop">
    Select your vCPU count, RAM, storage, operating system, and deployment region using the interactive configurator.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Desktop provisioned in 8 minutes">
    WinDesk Cloud automatically provisions your virtual machine, installs the operating system, and configures secure access. You'll receive a connection link when it's ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect from anywhere">
    Access your desktop from any browser or supported client. All traffic is encrypted end-to-end via the zero-trust network layer.
  </Step>
</Steps>
